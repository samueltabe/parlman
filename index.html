About The Author

Manny Perlman

Founder & CEO, Destination Peace International | Peace Activist & Cultural Diplomat | International Correspondent | Clergy Emeritus & Psychiatric Social Worker Emanuel C. Perlman’s unique path to founding Destination Peace International emerged from five decades of diverse yet interconnected experiences. His 50 years of clergy service, culminating in Clergy Emeritus status, provided deep spiritual wisdom and interfaith dialogue skills essential for cross cultural peacebuilding. As a psychiatric social worker and hospital administrator, he developed expertise in crisis intervention, trauma healing, and therapeutic community management, critical foundations for addressing collective wounds in conflict zones.

About The Book

A Blueprint for Peace in a Divided World rep- resents the culmination of five decades of experience in conflict resolution, psychiatric care, and international peacebuilding. This groundbreaking work offers practical wisdom for building bridges across the divides that fracture our world, drawing from Emanuel C Perlman’s unique synthesis of clinical expertise, spiritual insight, cultural diplomacy, and real world peacebuilding experience to provide readers with both inspiration and actionable strategies for creating positive change in their communities and beyond.
